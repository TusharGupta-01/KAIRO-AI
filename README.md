# 🚀 KAIRO AI
### Your Personalized Career Operating System

> AI-powered ecosystem that helps students plan, learn, organize, and achieve their career goals through personalized roadmaps, AI mentoring, and an intelligent knowledge system.

---

## 📌 Problem

Students today use multiple disconnected platforms for learning, notes, roadmaps, resumes, internships, and career guidance.

This fragmented workflow causes:

- No personalized learning path
- Generic AI advice with no context
- Scattered study resources
- Lack of progress tracking
- Poor career planning

---

## 💡 Solution

KAIRO AI brings everything together into one intelligent ecosystem.

Instead of using multiple apps, students receive a personalized AI-powered operating system that understands:

- Academic background
- Career goals
- Daily learning capacity
- Progress
- Projects
- Skills
- Learning resources

KAIRO continuously adapts guidance according to each student's journey.

---

# ✨ Features

## 🤖 AI Mentor

A context-aware AI mentor that understands:

- Student profile
- Career goals
- Personalized roadmap
- Previous conversations

Unlike general AI chatbots, responses are tailored specifically to each student.

---

## 🗺️ AI Roadmap Generator

Automatically generates a personalized learning roadmap based on:

- College
- Branch
- Semester
- Career Goal
- Timeline
- Target Companies
- Daily Study Hours

Each roadmap includes:

- Daily missions
- Learning tasks
- Estimated study time
- Career impact
- Expected outcomes

---

## 📚 Knowledge Vault

A centralized repository for learning resources.

Current MVP includes:

- Upload notes
- Organize resources
- Favorites
- Collections
- Resource management

Future versions will include:

- AI Search
- AI Summarization
- Smart Notes
- Auto Tagging
- Concept Linking

---

## 👤 Student Profile

One-time onboarding collects:

- Academic information
- Career goals
- Target companies
- Available study time

This profile powers every AI feature inside KAIRO.

---

## 📈 Progress Tracking

Track:

- Daily completion
- Roadmap progress
- Learning consistency
- Career journey
